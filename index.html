"Dump Trump"

Donald Trump represents an existential threat to our democracy and everything good about America. If you want to do everything you can as a citizen, to stand up for the American ideals we cherish, this app is for you.

In this app, users are able to post to a global list of reasons on why he should not be elected and also invite other users to the campaign. Users are awarded points for both of these actions. Every step is taken to protect user anonymity, including usernames, anonymous login, and the ability to delete your account at anytime.

Please enjoy!
